Lindy Murphy

Motivating women to engage with finance

Lindy Murphy

Lindy Murphy is a junior at American University majoring in both Economics and CLEG who loves to work and learn. Having a mom whose career has been defined by her financial expertise– ranging from investment management to digital assets– the importance of financial literacy has been clear to Lindy since she was a child. Throughout her time as an economics student, her classes have repeatedly discussed the financial literacy gender gap, striking in her an interest in understanding its causes. Eager to learn and get involved in the DC community in a truly meaningful way, when Lindy heard about Dollar Scholars from Professor Jeff Dickinson––it sounded like the perfect opportunity. Other than Dollar Scholars, Lindy is involved in American’s Business Law Society as well as the Sine Institute and (if she’s not on campus) she’s going for a run around DC!
